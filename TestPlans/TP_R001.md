# TP_R001 — Combat log scroll fix

**Slice:** R001 (regression fix)
**Goal:** The combat log no longer truncates in long combats. New lines auto-scroll into view; older lines are reachable by scrolling up.

Fill in **Actual** for every step. Mark each checkbox once verified.

## Happy path

- [ ] Step 1. Open `DungeonDebt/Assets/Scenes/Main.unity` in Unity Editor and press Play.
      Expected: Title "Dungeon Debt", "Ready" status, Start Run button enabled, log panel visible on the left with a vertical scrollbar on its right edge. No Console errors or warnings.
      Actual: pass

- [ ] Step 2. Click **Start Run**, then in the Shop hire any 2 heroes and click **Continue**.
      Expected: Combat resolves; log lines stream from top to bottom with the existing line delay. The view stays pinned to the most recent line (no lines disappear off the bottom).
      Actual: pass

- [ ] Step 3. While combat is streaming, scroll the mouse wheel **up** inside the log panel.
      Expected: The view scrolls upward and earlier log lines become visible. New lines continue to be appended.
      Actual: pass

- [ ] Step 4. After combat ends (Continue button visible), scroll the wheel up to the top of the log.
      Expected: The very first line (e.g. "Combat begins." or the first attack line) is reachable. No lines are missing between top and bottom.
      Actual: pass

- [ ] Step 5. Drag the scrollbar handle on the right edge of the log panel.
      Expected: The log scrolls in response to the drag. Handle position tracks the visible portion of the log.
      Actual: pass
